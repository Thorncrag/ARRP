# HOR-044 — Statutory Framework for Presidential Cross-Border Infrastructure Permits

> **Source-development record:** This record assembles source-specific research routed to the candidate. It is source-development work, not a completed project finding, admission decision, or determination that the described conduct is unlawful or institutionally defective.

Each entry states the proposition presently under review. `Reviewed: No` means that proposition still requires source-level verification or qualitative placement. A monitoring qualification identifies the future development being watched; it does not establish that the source presently supports a final conclusion.
