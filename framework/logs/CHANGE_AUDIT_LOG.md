# Change Audit Log

> **Historical record.** This cumulative log is preserved for provenance through July 20, 2026. Future Change Audits do not append here: proposal-specific findings remain in their issue audit sidecars, while durable cross-project lessons are incorporated in the governing file, validation script, or test that owns the rule.

This page is the cumulative log for project-wide Change Audits and Project Consistency Audits. It preserves governing-rule, template, scoring, and structural-consistency changes that affect how issue pages, audit histories, inventories, and GitHub Project fields should be interpreted.

Proposal-specific Change Audits are recorded in the affected proposal's sibling audit-history file, not duplicated here, unless they also change a governing rule, template, schema, rubric, or cross-project convention.

Related changes made in rapid succession should be collapsed into one coherent log entry when doing so improves readability and still preserves the material changes, affected files or proposal, score or rebaseline effect, unresolved findings, and reason the edits are treated as one change set. Every logged Change Audit belongs in the standard table; do not place same-audit supplements, reruns, reconciliations, or explanatory exceptions as free-standing prose above or below it. Fold those details into the original row when practical or use one consolidated standard row when they materially exceed the original row's scope.

Current cross-issue score, audit-status, and next-audit tracking lives in the [ARRP GitHub Project](https://github.com/users/Thorncrag/projects/2). Change Audit rules are maintained in [Project Methodology](../METHODOLOGY.md#change-audit).
