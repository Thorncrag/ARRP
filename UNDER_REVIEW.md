# Issues Under Review

ARRP investigates possible institutional weaknesses before deciding whether they warrant a proposal. This page lets readers see which subjects are already being researched, developed, held for a defined reason, or monitored for an external development.

Appearing here does **not** mean that ARRP has concluded that misconduct occurred, that existing law is inadequate, or that a new reform should be adopted. A candidate may ultimately be admitted, narrowed, merged into another issue, deferred, retired, or found to be adequately addressed by existing law.

## Request an Issue or Submit New Evidence

If your concern is not listed, ask ARRP to review it. If it is already listed, you can still contribute a new source, a missing example, a materially different institutional weakness, or a better remedy.
