## Audit History

### 2026-07-20 — Horizon candidate admission and source-development review

**Audit status:** In development; no proposal-quality score assigned

**Proposal-quality score:** 0

**Runs:** No change. This admission and source-development review is not a T0–T4 issue-quality audit.

**Admission finding:** HOR-032 was admitted independently as OVS-009 after review established a recurring, cross-administration enforcement gap for federal political-activity restrictions involving some senior officials. Existing law permits OSC investigation but may substitute presidential referral for independent adjudication, while OSC's treatment of White House commissioned officers changed between 2024 and 2025.

**Placement finding:** A-09 is the primary home because the asserted defect concerns oversight and enforcement architecture. Election administration, campaign finance, internal ethics advice, and general civil-service protections remain with ELEC-001, ELEC-012, OVS-008, and CIV-001 respectively.

**Scope finding:** OVS-009 does not prohibit ordinary political advocacy by administration officials, presume that every alleged violation occurred, or target one party. Any remedy must use neutral conduct rules, operate reciprocally, preserve due process, and avoid displacing constitutional appointment and removal authority.

**Next review need:** Complete the statute-first review of 5 U.S.C. chapters 12 and 73, MSPB jurisdiction, judicial review, OSC's former-employee authority, covered categories, exclusions, sanctions, and constitutional limits. Then select and draft the least-complex adequate vehicle using existing institutions where possible.
