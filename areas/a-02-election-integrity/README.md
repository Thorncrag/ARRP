# A-02 — Election Integrity

## Generalized Institutional Concern

The election system remains vulnerable to partisan interference, delegitimization of lawful results, pressure on election officials, disruption of certification, and efforts to obstruct the peaceful transfer of power.

- **ELEC-001** — Partisan interference with election administration _(status: Candidate; priority: High)_
- **ELEC-002** — Pressure on certification and canvassing officials _(status: Candidate; priority: High)_
- **ELEC-003** — Insufficient protection for election workers and officials _(status: Candidate; priority: High)_
- **ELEC-004** — Abuse of post-election litigation and investigatory mechanisms _(status: Candidate; priority: High)_
- **ELEC-005** — Weak safeguards against alternate-elector or certification schemes _(status: Candidate; priority: High)_
- **ELEC-006** — Insufficiently automatic presidential-transition procedures _(status: Candidate; priority: High)_
- **ELEC-007** — Federal or military interference with election administration _(status: Candidate; priority: High)_
- **ELEC-008** — Weak accountability for efforts to overturn lawful results _(status: Candidate; priority: High)_
