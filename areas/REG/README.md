# A-17 — Independent Agencies and Regulatory Neutrality

## Generalized Institutional Concern

The constitutional and statutory position of independent agencies is increasingly unstable, while presidential control over removal, enforcement, adjudication, funding, and rulemaking may compromise functions intended to operate with some insulation from immediate partisan command.

## Issue Boundaries

- [REG-001](issues/REG-001.md) is the primary home for *Trump v. Slaughter* and the erosion of statutory for-cause removal protections for independent multimember agencies. The opinion should be read alongside [*Trump v. Cook*](https://www.supremecourt.gov/opinions/25pdf/25a312_5468.pdf), which preserved a narrower Federal Reserve-specific interim protection lane grounded in central-bank history and statutory pretermination process.
